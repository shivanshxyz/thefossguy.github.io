F.A.Q

  • What is the eligibility criteria?

    A team should consist of undergraduates and/or school students from grades 11th/12th. At most 4 members are allowed in a team. Lone wolves are welcome too.

  • How much is the cost for attending?

    This event is completely free! We’ll provide you with a plethora of resources and tools to hack with.

  • What should I bring to the event?

    You need to carry your college/school ID Card, laptop, phone, chargers, any sleeping equipment you might want and a geeky t-shirt!

HackPhoria is a software hackathon event where developers, students, hobbyists participate to engineer an innovative application revolving around the themes provided a day before the event. The flagship event of Uphoria’20, HackPhoria is an overnight hackathon fuelled by creativity, passion, grit and coffee with prizes for winners and Best X ( X=Design, Best Pitch etc. TBD).
